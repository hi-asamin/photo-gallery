04 / ABOUT

Asami

神奈川生まれ。風景と光、ささやかな日常を撮影する写真家。
自然光のなかでの静謐な瞬間と、その手触りを写真に残すことを大切にしています。

Based in Kanagawa, Japan. Working with natural light and quiet landscapes since 2019. Featured in STUDIO VOICE, IMA, and selected exhibitions in Tokyo and Berlin.

  • 2025 Solo exhibition “Hour of Blue”, Tokyo
  • 2024 Group show, Berlin Photo Week
  • 2023 First photobook Stillness published
